THE BINDING

The Cursed Academy, Book 1

She came to prove herself. She stayed because of him.

When Lyra Ashford receives an invitation to the prestigious Veranthos Academy, she thinks it's her chance to escape her past and master the magic that's always been just out of reach.

She didn't expect Damian Vane.

Cold, arrogant, and impossibly skilled, Damian is everything Lyra hates—and everything she can't stop thinking about. Their rivalry ignites the moment they meet, and the academy walls seem to crackle with tension whenever they're near.

But something is wrong at Veranthos. Ancient magic pulses beneath the stone. Students are disappearing. And Lyra keeps hearing a voice that isn't a voice—a whisper from the curse that runs through her blood.

When a forbidden ritual goes wrong, Lyra and Damian are bound together by magic neither of them controls. Now their pain is shared, their thoughts bleed through, and the academy's darkest secret is unraveling.

To survive, Lyra must trust the one person she sworn to hate. To break the curse, she must face the truth about her past.

But some bonds are meant to break. And others are meant to last.

Dark Fantasy Romance. Enemies-to-Lovers. Fated Mates. Perfect for fans of From Blood and Ash and The Academy of Magic.
