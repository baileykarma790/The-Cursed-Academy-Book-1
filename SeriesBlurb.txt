The Cursed Academy
A Dark Fantasy Romance Series

---

Four books. One curse. A love that could destroy everything.

Lyra Ashford never wanted to be special. She just wanted to survive.

When she's summoned to the elite Veranthos Academy — a school for those born with forbidden magic — she finds herself at the center of a prophecy she never asked for. Ancient curses. Hidden bloodlines. And Damian Vane, the arrogant heir who makes her blood boil and her heart race in equal measure.

They were supposed to be enemies.

Instead, a failed ritual binds them together — sharing pain, thoughts, and a connection neither of them can escape. As the academy's darkest secrets unravel, Lyra must uncover the truth about her past, face the forces hunting her, and decide whether the bond between her and Damian is a curse… or a salvation.

The Cursed Academy is a four-book dark fantasy romance series perfect for fans of:
- From Blood and Ash
- The Academy of Magic
- Enemies-to-Lovers
- Fated Mates
